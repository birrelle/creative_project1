Hi! my name is Emily Little, and I am currently a student at BYU in Utah. While I am not studying anything art related, I love to keep up on this hobby in my free time.

I have loved art ever since I was a child. I really enjoyed pencil and colored pencil drawing in high school, and have just recently joined the world of digital art. In the last year or so I have been inspired by so many wonderful artists around me, and I have decided to expand my art abilities and share this progress with others.

When I am not drawing or doing homework, you can find me somewhere outdoors or developing one of my many hobbies!
